# Risk Scoring Model

Risk Score = Impact × Likelihood

## Impact Scale (1–5)

1 = Minimal  
3 = Moderate  
5 = Severe  

## Likelihood Scale (1–5)

1 = Rare  
3 = Possible  
5 = Very Likely  

---

## Severity Classification

1–5 → Low  
6–10 → Medium  
11–15 → High  
16–25 → Critical  

---

## Example Calculations

Brute Force Attack:
Impact = 3  
Likelihood = 3  
Score = 9 → Medium/High  

Data Exfiltration:
Impact = 5  
Likelihood = 4  
Score = 20 → Critical
